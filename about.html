About
Photo by Richard Harris on Unsplash

We are Jane Flex and John Doe, and we started Blooms as a way to provide the healthiest local flowers for everyone to be confident that they are getting the best bouqets for their loved ones and special events. After years of experimenting with different kinds of organic growing methods and comfortable environments, we have settled on a formula that is guaranteed to bring out beautiful floral wonders for you.

More about Jane Flex

Female Head Silhouette Taken from Adobe Stock

I have had a deep passion for studying the visually and structurally complex design of flowers since my childhood. Each type has played its part in Earth's history with a significant message to convey, and I enjoy using them like a wordless language to display all sorts of meanings in the bouqets I design. When I am not spending my time near stems and petals, I usually camp, hike, and read books.


More about John Doe

Male Head Silhouette Taken from iStock

Painting has been my main focus for the majority of my career. It was not until 7 years ago I tried gardening during a search for a new skill to learn when I became entranced by the art of floristry. Since then, I've been using my experience from representing the world on canvas boards along with a simple knowledge of cultivating flowers to maintain colorful, vivid species clients would love. Outside of flowers, I love to travel, cycle, and do photography.
